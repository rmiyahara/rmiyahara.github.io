Hello world! My name is Ryan, I was born and raised in San Jose, CA. After graduating from Independence High School, I began college at University of California, Los Angeles. While I started off as a Civil Engineering major with no interest in coding, my first Computer Science class initiated a personal revelation. From then on, school began to have a new meaning to me. I am currently in my senior year with an expected completion date in December of 2019.

Outside of school, I enjoy finding practical applications for my code such as reminding me when birthdays are and keeping track of personal fitness goals. I also spend my time playing Super Smash Bros. Melee, Overwatch, and the Japanese Taiko drum.
